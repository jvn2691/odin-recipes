Lasagna Recipe

Check out our tasty lasagna recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 16 ounce package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Bring large pot of slightly salted water to boil. Add past and cook until al dente;drain.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Cook ground beef.
  3. Combine ingredients.
  4. Enjoy!
